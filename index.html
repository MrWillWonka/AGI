Upgrades

Hardware Physical components that increase compute power generation. Focus on hardware first to establish resource flow.
Software Programs that improve data collection and processing efficiency. Invest in these after basic hardware to enhance data operations.
Algorithms Advanced learning methods that unlock once Intelligence reaches 5. Crucial for accelerating research and developing AI capabilities.
Consciousness The final frontier of AI development. Unlocks when Awareness reaches 10. These upgrades are essential for achieving true AGI.
Basic CPU Upgrade +0.5 CP/s The most affordable hardware upgrade. Adds 0.5 CP per second for each level. Best early game investment - get a few levels of this first before moving to other upgrades.
Server Cluster +1 CP/s, +1 CP/click More powerful than CPUs but also more expensive. Adds 1 CP per second for each level AND 1 CP per click. Good second-tier upgrade after you have a few CPU levels.
Quantum Processor +5 CP/s Significantly boosts CP generation by adding 5 CP per second for each level. High cost but high return - a major milestone upgrade that accelerates mid-game progress.
Neural Network Chip +15 CP/s Advanced hardware that adds a massive 15 CP per second for each level. Very expensive but transforms your capability. Save up for this after establishing quantum processing.
Data Processor +1 DP per collection Increases data points gained per collection by 1. Very cost-effective upgrade that compounds with auto collection. Prioritize this early if you're actively collecting data.
Auto Data Collector Automatically collect data Automatically collects data points each second without spending CP. A game-changing upgrade that provides passive data growth. Invest in this as soon as you can afford it.
Training Optimizer +50% training speed Makes training 50% more effective, generating more Intelligence per training session. Multiplicative bonus that becomes more powerful as you upgrade further. Great after unlocking training.
Natural Language Processing +5 DP per collection, +1 Intelligence Significantly increases data collection efficiency and provides an immediate Intelligence boost. A powerful mid-game upgrade that accelerates progress.
Computer Vision +10 DP per collection, +2 Intelligence Advanced software that dramatically increases data collection and Intelligence. A major upgrade that helps transition to later game phases. Worth saving for after establishing NLP.
Machine Learning +1 RP per research, +1 Intelligence Foundation of advanced AI. Increases research efficiency and boosts Intelligence. Your first major algorithmic upgrade that opens the path to deeper learning.
Deep Learning +2 RP per research, +2 Intelligence More advanced learning algorithms that significantly enhance research capabilities. Unlocks reinforcement learning. A crucial mid-game upgrade that accelerates progress.
Reinforcement Learning +3 RP per research, +2 Intelligence, +1 Creativity Enables learning through trial and error. First upgrade to boost Creativity, which is essential for phase advancement. Invest after establishing deep learning capabilities.
Transfer Learning +5 RP per research, +3 Intelligence, +2 Creativity Apply knowledge from one domain to another. Massive boost to research and attributes. A transformative late-game upgrade that dramatically speeds up advancement toward AGI.
Self-Awareness Module +5 Awareness First step toward consciousness. Introduces the Awareness attribute, which is crucial for phase advancement. A fundamental upgrade for developing AGI.
Creative Synthesis +10 Creativity, +2 Awareness Enables creating novel solutions and ideas. Major boost to Creativity, accelerating phase progress. Unlocks metacognition. A key upgrade for advanced AGI development.
Metacognition +10 Intelligence, +5 Awareness Thinking about thinking. Massive boost to Intelligence and Awareness. Unlocks consciousness emergence. Close to a breakthrough in achieving true AGI.
Consciousness Emergence +20 Awareness, +10 Creativity, Major AGI progress The pinnacle of AGI development. Provides breakthrough advancements across multiple attributes and dramatically increases phase progress. The ultimate upgrade in your journey toward true artificial general intelligence.
